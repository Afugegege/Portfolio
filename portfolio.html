WHO AM I


I am Raiden Shogun (Japanese: 雷らい電でん将しょう軍ぐん Raiden Shougun), a playable Electro character in Genshin Impact.

I am comprised of two beings in one body: Ei, the current Electro Archon of Inazuma; and the Shogun, the puppet created by Ei to act as the ruler of Inazuma in her stead, which also serves as her vessel.
